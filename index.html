About Me

Hi, my name is Sharon Nguyen. I have 6-7 years of healthcare experience as a Certified Nurse Assistant witin the ED Psych Unit, and am currently transitioning out of healthcare into software development. I made this tough decision to sacrifice my current career to transition into the tech industry due to burn out during COVID-19, and because I saw how important software/programming tools have within the health realm.

I hope to merge my interpersonal skills, mission-driven passion for accessible healthcare, and patient advocacy into an app developer. It is my ultimate goal to create an FEMTECH app like Door Dash, that can help end period poverty by providing hygeine products to under-served communities.

I also have an interest in UX/UI design, AR/VR development, and video game development. If you would like to learn more about my journey with Year Up as an emerging professional, I can be found on LinkedIn and Github.
